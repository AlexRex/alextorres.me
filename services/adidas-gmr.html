adidas GMR: sensor data at scale

The problem

adidas GMR put a motion sensor inside a football insole. Every session produces streams of raw IMU data that have to be ingested, validated, processed and served back to a mobile game. It has to work reliably for a global user base.

What I worked on

The serverless data platform behind the product, built on AWS as dozens of small services and lambdas.

  • Ingestion and validation pipelines for sensor data, with protobuf/avro schemas as the contract between services.
  • Synchronization of sensor streams with video for labelling and algorithm development.
  • Supporting services: user management, notifications, campaign and session management, CI/CD tooling.

The result

A consumer product by a global brand, launched with EA and Google, and the experience of building data pipelines that can't afford to lose a single session.
